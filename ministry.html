Where can I get involved?


Get involved to our ministry work today to help towards the growth of our church. Get to explore some of our interest and/or talents with us by choosing the ministry that best suits you.

One person once said uninvolved people never reach spiritual maturity.

Here are some different ministries that you can choose from:





Band

This ministry inculde the choir, vocalist, instrument, sound engineering and music director








Media

Are you passionate with presenting? Well, this field if for you.

We are looking for people who are going to work with cameras, managing our social media platforms, graphic design, lyrics on the screen and lighting control.



Ushers and Deaconess

These are the people that will be helping inside the church to ensure that it is clean, and well organized so that it becomes a conducive environment for believers to fellowship together.

Other roles include fostering control and discipline during church services, assisting in alter calls and collecting offering buskets.






Sunday School

This fields works with Sunday school kids, ensuring that they are well taken care if during church services, taught the Words of God from young age and other values that must possess







Alter Workers

Every Sunday, we experience a number of people getting saved. To ensure that people people are in good hands, we need people to help at the alter then teach our new converts about salvation

and help guide them in their new journey. They will also equip people about the necessary information about our church, mission, vision, mandate etc.
