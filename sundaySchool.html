Sunday School







Importance:


Kids Church or Sunday School provides a nurturing environment for children to learn about the teachings of the Bible in a way that is engaging and age-appropriate.

It helps children develop a strong foundation of faith and moral values from a young age.

Through stories, songs, and activities, children can understand important lessons from the Bible and how to apply them in their daily lives.


Biblical Reference:


Proverbs 22:6 - "Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it."

Matthew 19:14 - "But Jesus said, Suffer little children, and forbid them not, to come unto me: for of such is the kingdom of heaven."


Why Kids Should Come to Church:


Spiritual Growth: Children can grow spiritually and develop a personal relationship with God.

Community: They can be part of a supportive community of other children and adults who share their faith.

Values: Learn important values such as love, kindness, forgiveness, and empathy.

Education: Gain knowledge about the Bible and its teachings in an interactive and fun way.


Invitation to Parents:


We invite you to bring your children to Kids Church or Sunday School to experience a nurturing environment where they can learn about the teachings of the Bible in a fun and engaging way. By exposing them to these valuable lessons from a young age, you are helping them build a strong foundation of faith and values that will guide them throughout their lives.

Join us as we create a welcoming community for your children to grow spiritually, develop meaningful friendships, and learn important life lessons from the stories of the Bible. Together, let's provide them with the opportunity to explore their faith and discover the love of God in a supportive and caring environment.
