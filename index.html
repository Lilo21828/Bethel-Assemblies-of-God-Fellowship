Welcome to our Church

Empowered by the HOLY SPIRIT and passion for mentoring christians... Mathew 28:18-20

Welcome to Bethel Assemblies of God Fellowship!


We’re delighted that you’ve found your way here. Whether you’re a longtime believer or simply curious, our doors are open to you. At Bethel, we’re more than a church; we’re a family—a community of worshipers, prayer warriors, and hope restorers.





Mission

The mission of the church is to make disciples of non-disciples.


Baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.


Teaching them to obey all that Jesus commanded. The church exists to participate in God’s mission, revealing and serving “the mystery of God’s will” in the world.

Vision

“To see people from diverse backgrounds come together through Jesus, united in worship and obedience”

Mandate

Declaring the gospel of Jesus Christ.


Gathering disciples into worshiping communities.


Equipping believers to follow Jesus now and for eternity



Our Core Values:


In the heart of our fellowship, you’ll find:



Worship:

We gather to lift our voices and hearts in worship, recognizing that God is worthy of all praise. Through music, prayer, and reflection, we seek to encounter His presence.


Hope Restoration:

Life can be challenging, and sometimes hope seems distant. At Bethel, we believe that God’s love and grace can restore hope even in the darkest moments. We are here to walk alongside you, offering encouragement and support.


House of Prayer:

Our church is a house of prayer, where we intercede for our community, nation, and the world. We believe in the power of prayer to transform lives and circumstances.


Caring Community:

Bethel is a place where people matter. We care for one another, share burdens, and celebrate victories. Whether you’re new or have been with us for years, you’re part of our family.


Biblical Teaching:

We strive to teach, lead, and guide people into the Word of God. Through relevant preaching and Bible studies, we equip believers to live out their faith in practical ways.


Building Relationships:

Our ultimate goal is to build strong relationships—with God and with each other. We encourage fellowship, discipleship, and genuine connections that go beyond Sunday services.
